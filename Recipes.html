Pasta Primavera

Ingredients:

  1. 8oz (225g) pasta (spaghetti or penne)
  2. 2 cups mixed fresh vegetables (bell peppers, cherry tomatoes, broccoli, and carrot)
  3. 2 cloves garlic, minced
  4. 2 tablespoons olive oil
  5. 1/4 cup grated Parmesan cheese
  6. Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions:

  1. Cook the pasta according to package instructions until al dente. Drain and set aside.
  2. In a large skillet, heat olive oil over medium heat. Add minced garlic and sauté for a minute.
  3. Add the mixed vegetables and sauté for about 5-7 minutes or until they are tender.
  4. Combine the cooked pasta with the sautéed vegetables. Toss well.
  5. Season with salt and pepper, and sprinkle with Parmesan cheese before serving.

Black Bean Quesadillas

Ingredients:

  1. 1 can (15 oz) black beans, drained and rinsed
  2. 1 cup corn (fresh or frozen)
  3. 1 cup shredded cheddar cheese
  4. 4 large flour tortillas
  5. 1 teaspoon cumin
  6. 1/2 teaspoon chilli powder
  7. Salt and pepper to taste
  8. Salsa and sour cream for dipping (optional)

Instructions:

  1. In a bowl, combine black beans, corn, cumin, chili powder, salt, and pepper.
  2. Place a tortilla in a dry skillet over medium heat. Add 1/4 of the bean mixture and 1/4 of the shredded cheese on one half of the tortilla.
  3. Fold the other half of the tortilla over the filling, creating a half-moon shape.
  4. Cook for 2-3 minutes on each side until the quesadilla is golden and the cheese is melted.
  5. Repeat for the remaining tortillas and filling.
  6. Serve with salsa and sour cream if desired.


Stir-Fried Chicken & Veg

Ingredients:

  1. 2 boneless, skinless chicken breasts, cut into thin strips
  2. 2 cups mixed vegetables (bell peppers, broccoli, snap peas, carrots)
  3. 2 tablespoons soy sauce
  4. 1 tablespoon olive oil
  5. 1 tablespoon honey

  8. Cooked rice or noodles for serving

Instructions:

  1. In a small bowl, whisk together soy sauce, honey, ginger, and garlic.
  2. Heat olive oil in a large skillet or wok over high heat.
  3. Add chicken strips and stir-fry for 3-4 minutes or until cooked through.
  4. Add the mixed vegetables and continue stir-frying for an additional 4-5 minutes.
  5. Pour the soy sauce mixture over the chicken and vegetables. Stir well to coat.
  6. Serve over cooked rice or noodles.
